Delivery Load Planner

Delivery load planner is a free and open source tool for small logistic companies who operate temperature controlled deliveries, with multiple destinations per tour on daily basis.

Delivery load planner(dlp) povides you overview of your reefers/trucks, clients delivery locations and driver personal.
You can register Delivery plans with clients destination locations and in right sequesnce. Delivery plans should be replanned and registred every quarter, to optimize your delivery quality.
Every delivery plan can be executed once a day, and registred as a Tour.
Tour bounds a driver and a truck/reefer, those will not be able to take any other tour on that day. Every tour's delivery point is a registred clients address, it has a expected delivery time and registred number of pallets. Pallets are temperature controlled items that are Frozen, Chilled and Dry goods. Frozen goods have to be transposted under -20 C, while Chilled and Dry goods can be transported under tempera as low as +5 C.
Every tour will be loaded into a reefer, with multiple temperature control zones. The load/unload plan is displayed on the tour page with along with table of delivery details. Load/unload plan schematic display the pallets inside the reefer/truck, Frozen goods are separated from Chilled and Dry goods, each covering one or more temperature controll zones.

There are two types of users in dlp(Delivery load Planner):

  • Planner has to be virified by admin.
  • Driver has to be virified by Planner.

For drivers: proceed to registration, after you register a user, please provide personal driver information and "driver id" given to you by your employer.
